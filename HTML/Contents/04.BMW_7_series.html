“The new BMW 7 Series represents our take on contemporary luxury. We consider technical innovation and a high quality of all details as indispensable as the emotional form language and the atmosphere of wellbeing of the interior. During the design process, we worked with the idea of ‘Technological Poetry’ – the feeling of a perfect interaction between progressive technology and its translation into design.”

Karim Habib, Head of Design BMW Automobiles





EXTERIOR DESIGN.


Dynamic proportions, precise lines and sculptured surfaces: The BMW 7 Series shows typical BMW design in its most elegant form. Be it in the forward-surging front, the extended side view or powerful rear, the BMW 7 Series radiates its natural and authoritative presence from every angle.

Ambient light

The special equipment ambient lighting provides gentle, stylish illumination. This underscores the luxurious ambience and also serves as a functional source of light, e.g. in the storage compartment of the centre console as well as in the door pockets and side lining. The colour switch function lets you create your preferred mood by giving you the choice between a bright white or muted orange light.

Multifunctional instrument display

The optional multifunctional instrument display stages the different driving experience modes with customised instrument displays – and thus creates worlds of colour and mood for more safety, efficiency and exemplary driver orientation. Navigation instructions and information from the driver assistance systems can now be registered even more easily at a glance. With these features, the new digital presentation distinctly increases the comfort, experience and safety of driving.

Bang and Olufsen high-end surround sound system

The BMW 5 Series Sedan is equipped with the innovative Bang & Olufsen high-end surround sound system. Supreme listening pleasure and a magnificent driving sensation merge into one unique experience. Sixteen active speakers with a total of 1,200 watts produce music in brilliant quality. Acoustic options can be selected: in ‘Studio’ mode, a clear sound emerges, while in ‘Expanded’ mode, the acoustic space appears enlarged, thus creating an incomparable surround-sound experience.

ACTIVE AIR STREAM KIDNEY GRILLE.

One uniquely distinctive design detail in the front view of the BMW 7 Series is the Active air stream kidney grille. The air vents with their slats in Chrome are visible and actively regulate the airstream. If the cooling demand for the engine increases, the air vents are opened. In the closed state they improve aerodynamics and ensure a striking presence due to the vertical slats in Chrome.

INTERIOR DESIGN.

Whichever seat you occupy in the BMW 7 Series, you feel the contemporary and future-orientated atmosphere of luxurious wellbeing. A virtuosic composition of material, light, shape, colour and innovative technologies to accompany people who wish to move things – be it from the rear or from the driver’s seat.

SPECIAL EDITION “MONTBLANC FOR BMW”.

Selected materials, an ingenious design and outstanding functions – contemporary luxury has many facets. The special edition “Montblanc for BMW” celebrates the passion shared by both brands for the utmost quality and craftsmanship. To mark this, Montblanc has developed a collection of selected accessories exclusively for the BMW 7 Series. The leather creations and writing utensils are perfect companions – for everyday use or for long, luxurious journeys. All items have a refined embossing in the BMW 7 Series style as well as a “Montblanc for BMW” signature. From spring 2016 onwards, the exclusive series will be available worldwide as part of the BMW Lifestyle Collection at selected BMW dealerships and in Montblanc boutiques.
