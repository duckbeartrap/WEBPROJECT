Dynamism that moves – even when standing still. The design of the BMW 4 Series Coupé radiates a fascinating athletic presence. The flat roof line and the forward-striving front of the vehicle give it a powerful elegance. The athletic rear ensures that the BMW 4 Series Coupé attracts glances in its wake as well. And the broad, self-assured stance signifies sheer driving pleasure. A promise that is not only fulfilled during the journey. But is far exceeded.


Seeming contradictions yield inspiring design: with its long wheelbase and short overhangs, the BMW 4 Series Coupé radiates self-assured authority. The rear-set greenhouse underlines the distinctive coupé style. And the flat roofline running dynamically to the rear and the presence of the vehicle demonstrate concentrated athleticism, well before the journey begins. Thrill to the innovative character of the BMW 4 Series Coupé in the following film.

Light meets speed: With the latest technology of Adaptive Full-LED Headlights, you’ll never again be left in the dark at night but will see every detail. Thanks to pure white colour of the light beam, similar to that of daylight, they ensure optimum and homogenous illumination of the road. This improves visibility and makes fatigue-free night-driving possible. What’s more, the light distribution adapts automatically to your speed and follows the steering angle so that nothing escapes your glance when cornering.




PROPELS YOU FORWARD. EVEN WHEN STANDING STILL.

Powerful. Agile. Typically BMW: the precise contours of the elongated bonnet give the BMW 4 Series Coupé the aura of classical yet innovative athleticism. The elaborately crafted double kidney grill in high-gloss black features nine broad slats. Like the innovative Full-LED Headlights, it is also extremely flat and has a distinctively broad design. As a consequence, the front of the BMW 4 Series Coupé creates an even more focused and powerful effect. Or in other words: the entire style of the BMW 4 Series Coupé has a charisma that promises sheer driving pleasure.




LEAVES AN IMPRESSION. WITH THE FOLLOWING TRAFFIC TOO.

A powerful statement from behind and from ahead: with its sporty, agile width, the rear of the BMW 4 Series Coupé makes for an impressive sight. The broad stance and flat roof line lend the vehicle an athletic perfection.
